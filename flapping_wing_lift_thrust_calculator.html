Flapping wing air craft thrust and lift calculator(Made using AI: Claude based on Res.Paper: https://doi.org/10.1109/ROBIO49542.2019.8961808)

Based on Peters' state-space airloads theory with strip integration — as used in the HITHawk paper

Governing equations
Reduced frequency (ratio of flap timescale to convection timescale)
k = π·f·c / U

Theodorsen lift deficiency function — Jones rational approximation
F(k) = 1 − 0.165/(1+(0.0455/k)²) − 0.335/(1+(0.3/k)²)
G(k) = [0.165·0.0455/k]/(1+(0.0455/k)²) + [0.335·0.3/k]/(1+(0.3/k)²)

Mean lift — strip theory integrated over span, with 3D planform correction η
L̄ = ½·ρ·U²·(η·b·c)·2π·sin(α)·|C(k)| |C(k)| = √(F²+G²)

Mean thrust — Garrick-type propulsion minus parasitic drag
T̄ = π·ρ·c·b²·Φ²·f²·F(k) − ½·ρ·U²·(η·b·c)·C_D0

η ≈ 0.80 for a tapered bird-like planform (accounts for 3D tip losses and camber). Valid for k < 1 and small flexible deformation (flap freq ≤ 5 Hz for a bird-scale FAV). Thrust accuracy is lower than lift — see paper section IV.
